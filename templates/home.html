ABOUT US

Our Vision

To be the premier finance professional association in Africa, offering world class opportunities to the members towards the exploration and exploitation of their academic and professional pursuits.

Our Mission

To develop ourselves into professionals with diverse skills and provide opportunities for self-fulfilment.

Our Mantra

Our mantra is: “Broadening Horizons”. In line with this we endeavour to plan for a number of activities that will focus us on our mission and lead us to the attainment of our goals.

  • Our core values that strongly bind us as an association are:


    Professionalism


    Teamwork


    Innovation


    Partnership


    Continuous Improvement
